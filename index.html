About Our Program

Our foreign language learning program is designed to help students master the basics to intermediate levels in both Arabic and English.

The learning focuses on speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills with an interactive approach and direct practice.

Course Objectives:
  • Improve everyday conversation skills
  • Understand grammar and important vocabulary
  • Prepare students for language exams or academic needs

Our Language Courses

Choose between our comprehensive Arabic and English programs

Arabic Language Program

Course Content:
1. Introduction to Hijaiyah Letters and Harakat

Students learn how to write and pronounce them

2. Basic Vocabulary

Starting with numbers 0-100, various colors, family member names, names of objects around

3. Everyday Conversation

Starting from introductions, greetings in Arabic, question words, conversations in class, canteen

4. Grammar

Fundamental Arabic grammar rules

5. Reading Short Texts

Developing reading comprehension skills

Learn More

English Language Program

Course Content:
1. Introduction and Greetings

Basic self-introduction, greetings and responses, asking for help

2. Basic Grammar

Tenses and pronouns, simple present tense, pronouns, to be

3. Daily Conversation

Topics: family, hobbies, travel

4. Reading Comprehension

Developing reading skills with various texts

5. Writing Simple Essays

Learning to express ideas in written form
